Notes:

  1. Bloom + Electro = Hyperbloom
  2. Bloom + Pyro = Burgeon

  5. Quicken + Hydro = Bloom
  6. Quicken + Pyro = Burning

  8. Bloom Cores explode when Hyperbloom or Blurgeon is activated, or after being on field for six seconds
